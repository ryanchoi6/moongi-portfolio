After hours I design, build, and direct — producing AI videos, websites, brand experiences, and content strategies for clients in real estate, beauty, and hospitality. My only employee is AI.

I'm bilingual (Korean · English), obsessed with visual storytelling, and convinced the best creative work of this decade will come from people who can hold both worlds — technical and artistic — in the same hand.
